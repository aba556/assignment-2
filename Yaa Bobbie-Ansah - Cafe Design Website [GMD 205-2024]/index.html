About

The owner's love for reading, drinking good coffee, and relaxation inspired them to start Cozy Bean Café. Finding a café that could provide all these three proved quite challenging! This inspired her to start her own café and provide this experience to anyone looking to relax and slow down from the fast-pace of every day life. Our comfy furniture including massage chairs, sofas, rocking chairs, hammocks and more, our catalogue of great books and our delightful menu options will provide a cozy and relaxing environment for you. The high quality of our coffee begins from the farmers and pickers who pick the coffee crop at the correct stage of ripeness, to the quality of our beans, to the roasting process.
